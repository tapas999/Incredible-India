About Kerala

Goa is a vibrant coastal paradise known for its golden beaches, Portuguese heritage, and lively nightlife. From serene churches to bustling flea markets, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Best Destinations

  • Baga Beach
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  • Old Goa (Velha Goa)
  • Anjuna Beach & Flea Market

Also explore Chapora Fort, Spice Plantations – Ponda, Anjuna Beach & Flea Market.

Famous Cuisine

  • Goan Fish Curry (Xitti Kodi)
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Sorpotel
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Goan Pao (Bread)

Don’t miss Bebinca , Caldine Curry, Goan Sausage (Chorizo) !

Popular Markets

  • Anjuna Flea Market
  • Arpora Saturday Night Market
  • Mapusa Market
  • Panjim Market
  • Margao Municipal Market
