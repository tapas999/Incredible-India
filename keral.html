About Kerala

Kerala famously known as "God's Own Country," captivates visitors with its serene backwaters, pristinebeaches, lush hill stations, and vibrant cultural exprienes. It's a holistic destination offering everything from Ayurvedic wellness to thrilling wildlife encounters.

Best Destinations

  • Munnar – Tea Gardens & Hills
  • Alleppey (Alappuzha) – The Backwater Paradise
  • Wayanad – Nature & Wildlife
  • Thekkady – Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Also explore Kumarakom – Serenity on Vembanad Lake , Kochi (Cochin) – Cultural Gateway, Vagamon – Misty Hills and Varkala – Cliffs & Beach Vibes.

Famous Cuisine

  • Sadya – The Traditional Feast
  • Kerala Fish Curry
  • Appam with Stew
  • Kerala Chicken Roast
  • Malabar Biryani (Thalassery Biryani)

Don’t miss Parotta & Beef Fry, Pazham Pori (Banana Fritters), Ulli Vada (Onion Fritters), and Kerala-style Filter Coffee & Spiced Tea!

Popular Markets

  • Broadway Market – Kochi
  • Spice Market – Mattancherry, Kochi
  • Chalai Market – Trivandrum
  • Connemara Market – Trivandrum
  • SM Street (Sweet Meat Street) – Kozhikode

Perfect for handicrafts, leather shoes, sarees and Broadway & Ernakulam Market,lulu mall and local food markets.
