About Jaipur

Jaipur, the "Pink City" is a captivating blend of ancient history and mordern vibrancy. Its tourism thrives on magnificent forts like Amer, majestic palaces such as the City Palace and Hawa Mahal, and bustling markets offering traditional crafts.

Best Destinations

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar

Also explore Jal Mahal, Albert Hall Museum, Birla Mandir and Lal Mahal Palace.

Famous Cuisine

  • Dal Baati Churma
  • Pyaaz Kachori
  • Laal Maas
  • Ghewar
  • Ker Sangri

Don’t miss Mawa Kachori, gatte ki sabzi!

Popular Markets

  • Johari Bazaar
  • Bapu Bazaar
  • Chandpol Bazaar
  • Triolia Bazaar
  • Neheru Bazaar

Don’t just stick to the main streets venture into the smaller lanea for unique finds and local workshops.
